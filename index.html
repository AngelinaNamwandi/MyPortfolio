My Design Process

Research & Discovery

I start by understanding your users, business goals, and market landscape. Through user interviews, competitor analysis, and stakeholder workshops, I gather insights that inform every design decision.

Ideation & Strategy

Based on research findings, I develop user personas, journey maps, and information architecture. This phase involves brainstorming solutions and creating a strategic roadmap for the design.

Design & Prototype

I create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes using Figma. This iterative process involves constant refinement based on feedback and usability testing.

Test & Iterate

User testing validates design decisions. I conduct usability sessions, analyze feedback, and iterate on the design to ensure optimal user experience before handoff.
